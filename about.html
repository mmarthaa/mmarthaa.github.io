About Me

One thing that has always surrounded me was art.
I have always had an inclination to creating work that engages the viewer and questions their ethics.
I started when I was about 7, and now I am 23 with a strong passion to continue making art.
My medium of choice is photography.
The more conceptual the better, is what I always strive for. When I am creating work, I always put pieces of myself in it.
My work is very personal to me, so each photograph is essentially a small window into who I am as a person.
